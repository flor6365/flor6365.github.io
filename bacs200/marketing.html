Ascent Archery

What is Ascent Archery?

Ascent Archery is an Archery Team that is part of a program known as JOAD (Junior Olymipic Archery Development). The purpose of this team and organization is to teach children and young adults the skills of shooting a bow and arrow with safety and with proper form. They then have the choice to take what they have learned and practiced and go to competitions to compete with others in their age range and bow type division.

What is the Age Range for archers in JOAD?

The age range for eligible archers are ages 6-20 years old. The archers will age out of the program the year of their 21st birthday.

What are the different bow divisions?

  • Compound
  • Bowhunter
  • Olympic Recurve
  • Barebow

NOTE: Coaches will go over the bow prior to registering for a tournament to make sure equipment is within the rules and regulations of the tournament.

How much is it to join?

The prices to join each team is different when it come to prices and memberships. Ascent Archery's fees for joining are as follows:

  • In order to join Ascent Archery, you will need to have a Greeley Archers Membership which is $90 per year.
    • Memberships are good for one year beginning February 1st and expiring January 31st.
    • The labor of managing and maintaining the range is all done by volunteers. If you participate in a workday, then you will be given a $25.00 credit to your next yearly dues. With a work credit dues are $65.00. The dates/times of workdays will be posted under events.
  • Ascent Archery currently does not have any fees for joining, you just have to be a member with the Greeley Archers.
